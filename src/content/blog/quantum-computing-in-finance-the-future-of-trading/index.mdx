## What Is Quantum Computing?  

Quantum computing leverages the principles of quantum mechanics to perform calculations at speeds unimaginable with classical computers. Unlike traditional bits (0 or 1), quantum bits (qubits) can exist in multiple states simultaneously, enabling parallel processing.  

### Key Features of Quantum Computing:  
- **Superposition**: Qubits can represent 0, 1, or both at the same time.  
- **Entanglement**: Linked qubits can instantly influence each other, even over distances.  
- **Quantum Tunneling**: Allows qubits to explore multiple solutions simultaneously.

### 1. **High-Frequency Trading (HFT)**

### 2. **Portfolio Optimization**

### 3. **Fraud Detection and Risk Management**  
Quantum-powered models can detect anomalies and predict market crashes with higher accuracy, safeguarding investments.
